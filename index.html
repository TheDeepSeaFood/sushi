The Origin of Sushi

Sushi has a long and fascinating history that traces back to ancient Japan, where it originally began as a method of fermenting fish with rice. Over centuries, it evolved into the iconic dish we know today, combining vinegared rice with fresh fish, seafood, and vegetables. From its humble beginnings, sushi has become a global culinary tradition, celebrated for its balance of flavors, textures, and artistic presentation.
